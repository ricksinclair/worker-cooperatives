## Content Highlights

### States with Dedicated Worker Cooperative Statutes
The following states have specific worker cooperative corporation statutes (covered in detail in their respective guides):
- **California** - Worker Cooperative Corporation (AB 816)
- **Colorado** - Limited Cooperative Association + Employee Ownership Tax Credit
- **Connecticut** - Worker Cooperative Corporations Act (Chapter 599a)
- **Illinois** - Limited Worker Cooperative Association Act
- **Maine** - Worker Cooperative Corporation Act
- **Maryland** - Worker Cooperative Corporations (Title 5, Subtitle 6A)
- **Massachusetts** - Employee Cooperative Corporations (Chapter 157A)
- **Minnesota** - Cooperative Associations (Chapter 308A/308B)
- **New York** - Worker Cooperative Corporation (BCL Article 5-A)
- **Oregon** - Cooperative Corporation Act (ORS Chapter 62)
- **Pennsylvania** - Workers' Cooperative Corporation (Title 15, Chapter 77)
- **Rhode Island** - Workers' Cooperative Corporation (Chapter 7-6.2)
- **Vermont** - Worker Cooperative Corporation (Title 11, Chapter 8)
- **Virginia** - Worker Cooperative Corporation (2020 statute)
- **Wisconsin** - Cooperative Associations (Chapter 185)

### Each State Guide Includes
1. Quick Summary (unique state features)

4. Governance & documents guidance
